# DTR-NQ-WP-20260722-12 — E6 portfolio sequencing

## Objective

Test whether the frozen E6 signal stream can produce a better risk-adjusted portfolio through sequencing alone, without changing signal quality, sessions, weekdays, entries, exits, stops, targets or E6's 0.25 D1-ATR exclusion.

## Frozen arms

- **S0:** current global one-position-at-a-time sequencing.
- **S1:** first executable E6 signal per ETH market date.
- **S2:** global sequencing with a fixed 60-minute cooldown after exit.
- **S3:** independent Asia, London and New York sleeves, one position per sleeve, one-third normal risk per trade.

## Scope

- reproduce E6 exactly before comparison;
- reconstruct all arms from the complete causal E6-eligible signal stream;
- compare S3 only on risk-normalized portfolio returns and drawdowns;
- run fixed cost stress, yearly/session attribution, paired ETH-date inference and familywise correction;
- independently reconstruct every result and verify deterministic repetition.

## Restrictions

No signal filtering, threshold search, alternate cooldown, daily trade-count search, sleeve-weight optimization, dynamic risk allocation, interaction with previous filters, Pine change or deployment authorization.

## Completion gate

A durable report, machine-readable evidence, independent review, hashes, handover, status/roadmap/changelog/decision-ledger update and green CI.
