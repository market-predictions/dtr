# DTR-NQ-WP-20260722-14 — E6 fixed-fraction equity and execution-cost stress

## Objective

Quantify the historical account-growth path and resampled drawdown/tail-risk distribution of unchanged E6 under fixed-fraction current-equity risk and progressively worse execution costs.

## Frozen inputs

- Starting capital: USD 100,000.
- E6 signal, Tuesday–Friday calendar, Asia/London/New York sessions, exits and global one-position sequencing unchanged.
- Risk levels: 0.50%, 1.00%, 1.50% of current equity per trade.
- Execution scenarios:
  - normal: one tick slippage per side plus $2.25 commission per side;
  - moderate: two ticks slippage per side plus the same commission;
  - severe: four ticks slippage per side plus the same commission.
- Registered 304-trade E6 stream must reproduce exactly.

## Required outputs

- exact historical equity path for all nine risk/cost combinations;
- final equity, return, maximum drawdown, maximum dollar drawdown, longest losing streak and time under water;
- 20,000 date-block and 20,000 month-block resamples for every combination;
- final-equity and maximum-drawdown quantiles;
- probabilities of ending below starting capital and reaching 10%, 20% and 30% drawdown;
- independent reconstruction and deterministic repeat;
- plain-English report, review, changelog, decision ledger and handover.

## Decision boundary

This is a risk diagnostic. Historical results cannot authorize a live risk level, leverage increase, Pine deployment or production use.

## Stop rules

- Any E6 metric, ordering or input-hash mismatch stops execution.
- No risk level or cost case may be selected by optimizing the historical endpoint.
- No volatility scaling, drawdown throttle, fractional Kelly or dynamic sizing may be added after results.
