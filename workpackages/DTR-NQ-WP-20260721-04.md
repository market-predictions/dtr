## Versioned operational definition

Public CISD terminology is not sufficiently standardized for research use. This work package therefore defines two explicit, causal anchor contracts:

1. **Sequence anchor:** the open of the first candle in the most recent contiguous opposite-delivery body sequence after the sweep.
2. **Last-candle anchor:** the open of the last candle in that same opposite-delivery sequence.

For a bullish reversal, opposite delivery means bearish candle bodies and confirmation requires a later close above the selected anchor. For a bearish reversal, the definition is symmetric. Confirmation must occur after sequence completion and no later than the existing reversal entry decision.

## Scope

1. Detect contiguous opposite-delivery sequences after the reversal sweep.
2. Generate sequence-anchor and last-candle-anchor CISD confirmations without lookahead.
3. Expire every older sequence when a newer opposite-delivery sequence starts, including older confirmed state.
4. Partition all state by deterministic reset epoch.
5. Annotate every frozen reversal signal with CISD timing, anchor, sequence length, body displacement, and retest state.
6. Separate frozen-cohort from implementable portfolio-filter analysis.
7. Test a small predeclared anchor/timing/retest neighbourhood.
8. Attribute every removed and newly enabled portfolio trade.
9. Run chronological, cost, coverage, and neighbourhood checks.
10. End with an explicit promote, hold, or reject decision.

## Predeclared variants

- `CISD_OBSERVE`: annotate without filtering.
- `CISD_SEQUENCE_CONFIRM`: require sequence-anchor confirmation.
- `CISD_LAST_CANDLE_CONFIRM`: require last-candle-anchor confirmation.
- `CISD_SEQUENCE_RECENT_3`: require sequence confirmation within three five-minute bars of entry.
- `CISD_SEQUENCE_RECENT_6`: require sequence confirmation within six five-minute bars of entry.
- `CISD_SEQUENCE_RETEST`: require a post-confirmation touch of the sequence anchor before the existing reversal entry.

These variants are structural alternatives, not authorization to select the best in-sample line.
