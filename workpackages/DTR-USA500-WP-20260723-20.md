# DTR-USA500-WP-20260723-20 — USA500 baseline discovery programme

## Objective

Develop an instrument-specific Day Trader Rauf research candidate for the qualified Dukascopy `USA500.IDX/USD` one-minute bid-CFD proxy, starting from the unfiltered frozen core logic rather than inheriting NQ context or event filters.

## Evidence classification

This is exploratory retrospective model development on previously inspected 2022–2025 proxy data. It is not pristine out-of-sample evidence and cannot validate CME ES execution.

## Programme hierarchy

1. USA500 unfiltered core reference.
2. USA500 calendar/session-selected baseline.
3. USA500 context-enhanced challenger.
4. USA500 event-policy challenger.
5. NQ E6 transfer control.
6. NQ E6 no-FOMC transfer control.

## Stage 1 — frozen Monday × Asia factorial

The same core signal, execution, exit, gap and global sequencing logic is used in all arms.

- `P0_TUE_FRI_ALL`: Tuesday–Friday; Asia, London and New York.
- `P1_MON_FRI_ALL`: Monday–Friday; Asia, London and New York.
- `P2_TUE_FRI_NO_ASIA`: Tuesday–Friday; London and New York.
- `P3_MON_FRI_NO_ASIA`: Monday–Friday; London and New York.

The previously observed London concentration is prior knowledge only. It does not authorize London-only selection.

## Stage 1 promotion gate

A calendar/session arm may replace P0 only when it:

- remains positive at one and two ticks per side;
- improves return/drawdown by at least 15% or reduces drawdown by at least 20% without lower expectancy;
- is positive in at least three of four calendar years, with no year below -5R;
- does not derive more than 70% of positive net R from one year;
- has at least 250 sequenced trades or retains at least 65% of P0 trades;
- has a positive paired date-block mean difference; the uncertainty interval is reported and promotion is labelled exploratory if it crosses zero.

If no arm passes, P0 remains the USA500 calendar baseline.

## Stage 2 — bounded context challengers

Only fixed, previously defined non-proxy-tuned thresholds are tested against the selected calendar baseline:

- exclude compressed same-session initial ranges below the trailing one-third percentile;
- exclude entries within 0.25 prior-day ATR of the directional extreme (NQ E6 transfer);
- require sweep-to-entry path of at most 12 five-minute bars;
- require entry extension from the protected pivot of at most 0.35R;
- require BOS quality score of at least two of three;
- require structural clearance to TP1.

No threshold search is authorized. At most one single-factor context challenger may advance. Interactions are prohibited in this work package.

## Stage 2 promotion gate

The challenger must retain at least 60% of selected-baseline trades, remain positive at two ticks, improve return/drawdown by at least 15%, be positive in at least three years with no year below -5R, and show a positive paired date-block mean difference. Crossing-zero uncertainty results in `EXPLORATORY_CHALLENGER`, not validated promotion.

## Stage 3 — event policy

After the context arm is frozen, attribute official FOMC, CPI, NFP, monthly option-expiration Friday and quarterly roll/expiration cohorts. Event exclusions are tested one at a time with exact portfolio resequencing. No buffers, previous-evening blackouts or event-time threshold search are authorized.

An event rule may advance only as a risk-policy challenger when it removes at least eight trades, improves net R and return/drawdown, does not worsen two-tick expectancy, and its benefit is not attributable to a single event date.

## Costs and robustness

- Normal: one tick per side plus $2.25 commission per side.
- Moderate: two ticks per side plus the same commission.
- Severe: four ticks per side plus the same commission.
- Date-block and month-block resampling.
- Year, half-year, session and direction attribution.
- Exact changed-trade attribution after resequencing.

## Controls and restrictions

- Preserve NQ E6 and NQ E6 no-FOMC as external transfer controls only.
- Do not pool NQ and USA500 trades.
- Do not tune the core signal parameters, targets, stops, session times, cost assumptions or gap policy.
- No Pine, live sizing or deployment authorization.
- Raw Dukascopy data remains temporary private research material and is not committed.

## Independent verification

A separately implemented verifier must reproduce the selected arms, metrics, paired effects, promotion decision and deterministic hashes, and audit programme compliance against this work package.
