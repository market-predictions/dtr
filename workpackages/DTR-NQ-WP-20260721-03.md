# DTR-NQ-WP-20260721-03 — IFVG entry-confirmation ablation

## Status

`CLAIMED`

## Owner

ChatGPT autonomous development agent

## Goal

Determine whether a causal inversion fair value gap (IFVG) adds independent decision value to the frozen NQ gap-safe reversal candidate. The module must distinguish descriptive association from an implementable confirmation filter and must not retune the reversal baseline.

`DTR_PY_NQ_CANDIDATE_0_1_GAP_SAFE`

## Scope

1. Define bullish and bearish three-candle fair value gaps causally at the close of the third bar.
2. Define directional inversion only after a later close crosses the far boundary of the original gap.
3. Attribute IFVG state to every frozen reversal signal without future leakage.
4. Measure signal coverage, baseline-trade coverage, expectancy, net R, drawdown, session, weekday, direction and chronological-period effects.
5. Separate cohort analysis from portfolio-level filtering so position-overlap consequences remain explicit.
6. Test a small predeclared age/proximity neighbourhood rather than searching broad thresholds.
7. Preserve `gap_policy: reject_unsafe`; no IFVG may bridge a reset interval.
8. Add deterministic manifests, compact evidence, fixtures and independent review.
9. End with an explicit promotion, hold or rejection decision.

## Predeclared research variants

- `IFVG_OBSERVE`: annotate the frozen reversal population without filtering.
- `IFVG_CONFIRM_ANY`: require at least one directionally aligned IFVG after the sweep and no later than the entry decision.
- `IFVG_CONFIRM_RECENT_3`: require the most recent aligned inversion within three five-minute bars of entry.
- `IFVG_CONFIRM_RECENT_6`: require the most recent aligned inversion within six five-minute bars of entry.
- `IFVG_CONFIRM_RECENT_12`: require the most recent aligned inversion within twelve five-minute bars of entry.
- `IFVG_ZONE_TOUCH`: require a post-inversion touch of the IFVG zone before the existing reversal entry.

These variants test timing sensitivity; they are not an authorization to select the best in-sample result.

## Acceptance criteria

- Frozen gap-safe baseline remains reproducible.
- IFVG detection is causal and deterministic.
- Reset boundaries invalidate pre-reset FVG/IFVG state.
- Long and short fixtures prove directional symmetry.
- Cohort and portfolio analyses reconcile their different denominators.
- Every added or removed portfolio trade is attributable.
- Coverage loss is reported alongside performance change.
- Chronological development, validation and later-research results are explicit.
- Cost and parameter-neighbourhood checks precede promotion.
- Pinned Ruff and pytest pass on Python 3.11 and 3.12.
- Independent review records a final decision.

## Promotion rules

`PROMOTE_IFVG_CONFIRMATION` requires:

- positive or clearly improved expectancy in development, validation and later research;
- economically meaningful effect after coverage loss;
- a non-fragile three/six/twelve-bar neighbourhood;
- no dependence on one session, weekday or direction;
- acceptable cost stress;
- no unexplained portfolio trade differences;
- independent review approval.

Otherwise choose:

- `HOLD_FOR_FRESH_DATA`;
- `REJECT_NO_INCREMENTAL_VALUE`;
- `REJECT_OVERFIT`;
- `REJECT_IMPLEMENTATION_OR_DATA_RISK`.
