# Work Package — DTR-NQ-WP-20260722-09

## Title

Monday inclusion × Asia exclusion factorial research

## Dependency

Stacked on `DTR-NQ-WP-20260722-08 — Advanced Context Robustness` and its timing-corrected exploratory reference:

`DTR_CAUSAL_BAR_CLOSE_RANGE_SHIFT_MINUS1`

## Research question

Determine independently whether:

1. restoring Monday improves the strategy;
2. removing the Asia session improves the strategy; or
3. the two choices interact.

## Frozen design

Four arms were fixed before performance inspection:

- **A0:** Tuesday–Friday; Asia, London and New York.
- **A1:** Monday–Friday; Asia, London and New York.
- **A2:** Tuesday–Friday; London and New York only.
- **A3:** Monday–Friday; London and New York only.

The same factorial was repeated as a robustness diagnostic under frozen E5 and E6 context rules. No thresholds, entries, exits, risk settings, session windows or execution assumptions changed.

## Evidence requirements

- exact A0 regression;
- exact E5 and E6 regression;
- complete portfolio reruns, not subtraction of session aggregates;
- paired market-date block bootstrap;
- annual, cost, drawdown and opportunity-coverage reporting;
- independent metric reconstruction and contract audit.

## Scope restrictions

Historical diagnostic evidence only. No deployment, Pine change, sizing recommendation, threshold adjustment, weekday subset search or session subset search is authorized.
