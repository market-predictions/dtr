## Root cause

DTR-FX v0.1 produced two mechanism-level replications of `FX_B1_PREVDAY_LONDON_BOS_MID`, but they used different candidate-density and execution details. The branch implementation produced 55 trades; the clean-room mechanism replication produced 144. Only the branch implementation retains executable source, source hashes, complete trade ledgers, and byte-reproducible evidence.

The executable contract is therefore selected by provenance completeness, not by return. The 55-trade implementation is frozen unchanged after exact reproduction. The 144-trade result remains corroborative mechanism evidence only and must not be used as an interchangeable backtest.

## Frozen B1 mechanism

- Instrument: Dukascopy GBPUSD M1 BID/ASK.
- Signal prices: five-minute midpoint OHLC.
- Reference: high, low, and midpoint of the most recent prior London trading day with at least 900 active minutes; weekends and under-covered holidays are skipped exactly as in the frozen implementation.
- Entry window: 06:00 inclusive through 10:00 exclusive in `Europe/London`.
- Direction: first qualifying previous-day high or low sweep owns the session.
- Reclaim: sweep bar plus the next three five-minute bars.
- Confirmation: causal three-bar pivot followed by the frozen displacement/BOS close.
- Fill: next active one-minute ask open for longs or bid open for shorts, plus 0.1 pip adverse slippage.
- Spread gate: fixed 1.2 pips, inherited unchanged from the 2022–2023 development partition.
- Stop: beyond the sweep extreme by the larger of 0.5 pip and 5% of five-minute ATR.
- Minimum initial risk: 4 pips.
- Cost gate: estimated total execution cost no greater than 15% of initial risk.
- Target: midpoint of the same prior qualified London trading day, at least 1.2 times estimated total execution cost from entry.
- Management: side-correct BID/ASK stop and target, stop-first same-minute ordering, gap liquidation after more than 10 inactive minutes, maximum 240 minutes or 16:00 London.
- Commission: USD 3.50 per side per standard lot.
- One open B1 position at a time.
