## Strategic question

Does the post-hoc no-map short-only strength observed on the 2022–2025 NQ futures archive replicate without modification on disjoint Nasdaq-proxy history, or was it a sample-specific directional anomaly?

## Scientific boundary

The observation was discovered after inspecting the NQ validation result and is therefore not evidence by itself. This package converts it into one frozen hypothesis and tests it only on non-overlapping data:

- older Dukascopy USATECH proxy history from 2015 through 2021;
- a fresh 2026 YTD USATECH proxy partition ending before 2026-07-23.

The 2022–2025 USATECH proxy and the inspected NQ archive are excluded from candidate selection and promotion.

## Frozen candidate

- phase-one arm: `S123_M0_NO_MAP_CONTROL`;
- entry direction: short only;
- technical management: both directions retained;
- all Step 1, retest, base, Step 3, stop, maximum-hold and cost rules unchanged;
- no session or weekday filter.

## Comparators

- both-direction full sequence;
- long-only full sequence;
- causal short EMA-break-only;
- causal short EMA-break-plus-retest;
- fifty deterministic matched-time short controls.

## Source qualification gate

The source-only workflow must complete before any performance runner is added or executed. Each fixed annual/YTD partition must publish:

- deterministic compressed-file SHA-256;
- row count and source bounds;
- zero-volume removal count;
- duplicate and OHLC integrity audit;
- explicit proxy classification.

Expected checksums will then be committed into the frozen design. Any strategy workflow must reject missing or mismatched source hashes.

## Promotion screen

A proxy result can only be retained for paid actual-NQ validation if every predeclared gate passes, including:

- at least 500 older-history trades;
- positive older-history expectancy and positive date-block lower bound;
- at least five of seven older years positive;
- limited year concentration;
- two-tick and one-minute-delay resilience;
- at least 30 positive 2026 YTD trades with positive two-tick expectancy;
- incremental mechanism value over EMA break;
- matched-time coverage, holding-period and p95 superiority.

Passing this screen is not a strategy promotion. It only justifies purchasing or otherwise acquiring qualified unseen CME NQ data.
