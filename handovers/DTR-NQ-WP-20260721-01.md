## Comparison procedure

1. Confirm the reference manifest passes its frozen regression.
2. Compare `summary.json` metrics and period metrics.
3. Compare `funnel.csv`, including:
   - `sessions_range_gap_rejected`;
   - `sessions_signal_path_truncated`;
   - `skipped_unsafe_gap_bridge`;
   - `observed_unsafe_gap_bridges`.
4. Diff `trades.csv` by:
   - session;
   - session date;
   - direction;
   - entry timestamp;
   - exit timestamp;
   - exit reason;
   - PnL R.
5. Classify every missing or changed trade as one of:
   - contaminated session range;
   - reset during setup path;
   - unsafe gap during open trade;
   - position-overlap consequence caused by an earlier rejected trade;
   - unexpected difference requiring investigation.
6. Recalculate aggregate, half-year, session, weekday, and direction deltas.
7. Rerun both manifests a second time into clean output directories and verify deterministic artifact hashes.
8. Only then add expected baseline metrics to the gap-safe manifest.
