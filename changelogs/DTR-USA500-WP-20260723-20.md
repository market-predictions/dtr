# Changelog — DTR-USA500-WP-20260723-20

## v0.5.0-research.20 — 2026-07-23

### Changed

- Added a USA500-specific baseline-discovery programme starting from the unfiltered frozen DTR core.
- Added a preregistered Monday × Asia factorial.
- Added a separately preregistered session decomposition after the factorial produced no viable baseline.
- Added six fixed single-factor context diagnostics and five one-at-a-time event diagnostics.
- Added independent metric, sequencing, bootstrap and roadmap-compliance verification.

### Result

- No calendar/session arm passed the promotion gate.
- London-only produced +16.98R across 253 trades and remained slightly positive at two ticks per side, but was negative in 2022 and 2025 and failed concentration and uncertainty gates.
- No context or event rule created a viable cost-robust USA500 baseline.

### Reason

The NQ-derived configuration had only partial and cost-fragile transfer to the S&P proxy. An instrument-specific programme was required to distinguish universal logic from NQ-specific filters.

### Known limitations

- Dukascopy bid-CFD proxy, not CME ES futures.
- Retrospective 2022–2025 data had already been partially inspected.
- London-only remains regime-dependent and is not validated.

### Next

Acquire actual ES futures data or open a separately preregistered nested chronological ES-core development study. Do not continue neighboring threshold or interaction searches on this sample.
